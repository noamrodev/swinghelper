# Qullamaggie playbook (reference)

Kristjan "Qullamaggie" Kullamägi — Swedish momentum swing trader. Trades relative-strength leaders
with tight risk. Three core setups: **Breakout**, **Episodic Pivot (EP)**, **Parabolic Short**.

> Source notes: [3 timeless setups](https://qullamaggie.com/my-3-timeless-setups-that-have-made-me-tens-of-millions/),
> [Episodic pivots](https://qullamaggie.com/how-to-master-a-setup-episodic-pivots/). These are my
> understanding of his public method — correct anything here to match what I actually trade.

---

## Universe filters (apply to all setups)
- **ADR% > 4%** (≈ 5%+ preferred) — needs daily range to move.
- **Liquidity:** enough dollar-volume that I can get filled (avoid illiquid names).
- **Relative strength:** among the biggest gainers over 1 / 3 / 6 months.
- **Trend:** price above the **10-day and 20-day EMA** (and ideally 50-day SMA) for longs.

---

## 1) Breakout setup
The bread-and-butter. A leader makes a big move, rests in a tight base, then breaks out.

- **Prior move:** +30–100%+ over the last 1–3 months (the bigger/cleaner the leg, the better).
- **Consolidation:** 2–8 weeks of tightening range, contracting volume, holding above the 10/20 EMA.
- **Entry:** buy as price breaks the **high of the consolidation** (or high of the prior day on
  lower timeframes). Use a buy-stop just above the level.
- **Stop:** the **low of the breakout day/candle** or the consolidation low — whichever is tighter
  and ≤ 1× ADR. If a sensible stop is wider than 1× ADR, skip it.
- **Exit:** sell **1/3–1/2 into strength after 3–5 days** or at **2–3R**; move stop to breakeven;
  **trail the rest on the 10-day (aggressive) or 20-day (looser) MA** — exit on a daily close below it.

## 2) Episodic Pivot (EP)
A surprise that re-rates the stock — earnings or major news gap.

- **Trigger:** stock **gaps up ~10%+** on unexpected news/earnings with **huge volume**, ideally
  after being ignored/basing (not already extended).
- **Entry:** buy near the **opening-range high** (e.g. break of the first 1–5 min or 60-min high).
- **Stop:** the **low of the day** (or opening-range low), kept ≤ 1× ADR.
- **Exit:** same trim-and-trail logic as breakouts (10/20-day MA).

## 3) Parabolic Short — NOT USED (I trade longs only)
> I don't short. Never suggest this. Kept here only for reference.

- Stock is **massively extended** straight up (climax), often after a multi-day parabola.
- Short the reversal (e.g. first red day / loss of an intraday level like VWAP). Tight stop above
  the high. Higher risk — only surface if I ask.

## Position sizing & risk
- **Risk 1% of account per trade** (this overrides his 0.5–1% range for me).
- **Single position ≤ 10–20%** of account; not more than ~30% overnight across names.
- Bigger size comes from **tighter stops on lower timeframes**, not from risking more dollars.

## Quick checklist before any long
- [ ] ADR > 4%? 
- [ ] Above 10 & 20-day MA? 
- [ ] Clear prior momentum leg + tight base (breakout) OR fresh high-volume gap on news (EP)?
- [ ] Stop ≤ 1× ADR and at a real structural level?
- [ ] R to first target ≥ 2? 
- [ ] Not violating anything in journal/lessons.md?
