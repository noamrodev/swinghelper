# Pullbacks & AVWAP playbook (my favorite setups)

My preferred entries — favored over plain breakouts. The scanner scores these highest.

---

## 1) Continuation pullback (to the 10/20-day EMA)
A relative-strength leader that already moved, pulls back on lighter volume toward a rising moving
average, then resumes up. Lower-risk entry than buying the extended high.

- **Context:** uptrend intact — price above the 20 & 50-day MA, recent leader (+30%+ move).
- **Pullback:** 3–40% off the recent high, on **contracting volume**, holding at/near the
  **10-day EMA** (tight) or **20-day EMA** (deeper). Not extended above the 10 EMA.
- **Entry (preferred = limit into support):** the scanner sets the entry at the **rising support the
  stock is pulling back into** — nearest of the 10-EMA → 20-EMA → 50-MA (or the AVWAP). When price is
  still above that line, the entry is a **limit BELOW current price** (wait for the deeper dip — don't
  chase). Only when price has already reached/undercut support does it fall back to a **buy-stop on
  the reclaim of the prior-day high**. Confirm the bounce (reversal candle / lighter pullback volume).
- **Stop:** the **pullback swing low** (last ~7 days), capped at ~1.5× ADR. If the swing low is too
  far, use a 1.5× ADR stop.
- **Exit:** trim into strength at ~2–3R, then ride it and **exit on a daily close under the 9 EMA**.

## 2) AVWAP reclaim from the All-Time High
Anchor a VWAP at the **all-time high**. After a pullback, price that **holds / reclaims the
AVWAP-from-ATH** is institutional support resuming — buy there.

- **Setup:** price has pulled back to within ~1.5× ADR **above** the ATH-anchored VWAP and is
  holding it (not slicing through).
- **Entry:** reclaim / break of the prior day high while above the AVWAP.
- **Stop:** just below the AVWAP or the swing low (≤ ~1.5× ADR).

## 3) AVWAP reclaim from Earnings
Same idea, but anchor the VWAP at the **last earnings gap-up day** (the scanner proxies this as the
biggest recent gap). The post-earnings AVWAP is where buyers from the catalyst sit.

- **Setup:** price holding within ~1.5× ADR above the earnings-anchored VWAP after a pullback.
- **Entry / stop:** as above (reclaim of prior high; stop below AVWAP / swing low).

---

## Notes
- Breakouts and episodic pivots are still valid and the scanner still finds them — they just rank
  **below** pullbacks/AVWAP setups for me.
- All stops still respect the **1×–1.5× ADR** ceiling and **1% account risk** sizing.
- AVWAP from earnings uses a gap-based proxy for the earnings date (no fundamentals feed); verify the
  actual earnings date on the chart before trading it.
